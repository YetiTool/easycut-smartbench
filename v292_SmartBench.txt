Shapes, safety features, bug fixes and improvements
=======================================================
The Shapes app is now released! Find it in the console lobby.

New safety feature: SmartBench will now ensure the dust-shoe plug is inserted before starting a job (can be disabled via the maintenance app).

Various elements of the UI have been improved, including the probing screen and the go screen.

Support for Trespa added to YetiPilot.

Japanese is now available as a beta language.
**Find full release notes on our website, by scanning the QR code on the right.**
