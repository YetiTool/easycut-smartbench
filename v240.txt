Precision Pro + upgrade capability, and YetiPilot improvements
===============================================================

This release gives existing owners of SmartBench V1.3 the ability to upgrade their machines to Precision Pro + with an upgrade kit.

It also includes YetiPilot improvements, including a Spindle motor health check and an increased range of profiles. 

Not a Precision Pro + user? That’s ok, you’ll be seeing smarter features in the near future too!  

**Find full release notes on our website, by scanning the QR code on the right.**
