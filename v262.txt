New YetiPilot profiles for aluminium!
========================================

YetiPilot has been updated with 3 new profiles to cut aluminium.

Activated limit switches will now show up in bold and red on the status bar, so that you can more easily diagnose any issues caused by triggered limit switches.

**Find full release notes on our website, by scanning the QR code on the right.**
